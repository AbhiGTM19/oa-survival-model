About This System

The Tri-Modal Knee Osteoarthritis Prognosis System is an AI-powered clinical decision support tool that predicts the risk of disease progression and potential need for Total Knee Replacement (TKR) surgery.

How It Works

The system uses three modalities of data:

  • Clinical Data: Patient demographics, KL Grade, WOMAC Score
  • Biomarkers: COMP, CTX-II, HA, C2C, CPII levels
  • Imaging: X-Ray analysis using deep learning

Understanding Results

  • Risk Score (0-100): Higher values indicate greater risk
  • Risk Class: Low (<30), Moderate (30-70), High (>70)
  • Survival Curve: Probability of surgery over time
  • Heatmap: AI-detected regions of concern

Disclaimer

This is a research prototype developed for M.Tech thesis. It is not a certified medical diagnostic tool. All predictions should be verified by a qualified healthcare professional.
